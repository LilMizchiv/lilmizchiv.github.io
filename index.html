C.L. Fondal (Candice)

Vocals

C.L. Fondal resides in Denver, Colorado via Los Angeles, California. Sonically her vocal sound ranges from soul/R&B, gospel, hip-hop, pop, all the way to rock/blues. C.L. Fondal has performed at multiple music festivals and concert performances, providing an electrifying experience for all who attend. She has been singing on stage for over 20 years, creating a dynamic experience that connects with diverse audiences. C.L.'s vocal precision and incredible talent has become synonymous with a distinct musical style. She adds her vocal abilities to a group of experienced and incredibly talented musicians that make up the Oh Geez! Band.'

Miesha

Vocals

Miesha is a true vocal powerhouse residing in Aurora, Colorado, having brought her talents from her original home of Chicao, Illinois. Her musical journey began as a small child, gracing stages from small family gatherings and formal dinner parties to major venues across Chicago and Colorado. As a rare four-octave soprano, Miesha possesses a soulful yet fresh voice that shines across genres including R&B, Jazz, Pop, Soul, Rock/Blues, and has even been known to dabble in Opera. Dubbed "Aurora's Vocal Encyclopedia," Miesha is capable of effortlessly singing anything from Aaliyah to ZZ Top, providing the Oh Geez! Band with an incredible depth of sound and versatility.
